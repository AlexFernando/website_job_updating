    To guarantee your place, use the online booking form, which can be found at the CONTACT section.

    Show up 5 minutes before the start time:
    • Morning tour starts at 10h:00 daily.

    • Afternoon tour starts 15h00 daily.

    • Sun Lotion
    • Rain Jacket / Umbrella
    • Drinking Water
    • Coins (for photographs with locals & animals)
    • Camera / Video Camera
    • Wear Trainers / Walking Shoes
    • Sunglasses
